S.M.U.T.Sun Haven Museum Utility Tracker

Track Your Museum Donations

S.M.U.T. helps you track which items you've donated to the Sun Haven Museum and which ones you still need. With per-character saves and a clean UI organized by museum sections and bundles, you'll never lose track of your collection progress again.

Key Features

☑

Donation Tracking

Mark items as donated with a simple checkbox. Track your progress across all museum sections with visual indicators for completed bundles.

👤

Per-Character Saves

Each character has their own separate donation tracker. Switch between characters and your progress is always saved correctly.

📊

Progress Statistics

See completion percentages for each section, bundle, and your overall museum progress. Progress bars show exactly how close you are to completion.

🎨

Item Icons

Displays actual game item icons when available, making it easy to identify exactly which items you need to find.

🔍

Filter View

Toggle "Show only needed items" to hide already donated items and focus on what's still missing from your collection.

💎

Rarity Colors

Items are color-coded by rarity (Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary) so you can prioritize finding those rare collectibles.

How to Use

1

Open the Tracker

Press Ctrl + C to open the Museum Tracker window. Press Escape or the X button to close it.

2

Choose a Section

Click the tabs at the top to switch between The Hall of Gems, The Hall of Culture, and the Aquarium.

3

Expand Bundles

Click on any bundle header to expand it and see all the items within that category.

4

Mark Donations

Click the checkbox next to any item to mark it as donated. Your progress saves automatically!

5

Filter Items

Toggle "Show only needed items" to hide completed donations and focus on what you still need to find.

Installation

📦

1. Install BepInEx

Download and install BepInEx 5.x for Unity games if you haven't already.

📥

2. Download S.M.U.T.

Get SunHavenMuseumUtilityTracker.dll from Nexus Mods.

📁

3. Install the Mod

Place the DLL in BepInEx/plugins/SunHavenMuseumUtilityTracker folder.

🎮

4. Play!

Launch Sun Haven and press Ctrl+C to track your museum progress.
